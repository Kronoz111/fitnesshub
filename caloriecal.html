What is calorie?

Imagine that your body is like a car, and just like a car needs fuel to move, your body needs energy to do all the things you love to do, like playing, running, and even thinking. This energy comes from the food you eat, and we measure it using something called a "calorie."

So to GAIN, LOSE or MAINTAIN weight you have to keep track of your calories!

How does it work?

Its easy as 1,2,3!

  1. It is all about your carbohydrate intake! If you want to cut and LOSE weight, cut down on carbs and increase your protein intake! If you want to bulk and gain weight, INCREASE your carbs intake as well as your protein intake!

  2. Reduce your sugar and junk food intakes! Foods and drinks that are unhealthy gives you alot of calories! You can have a cheat day once or twice a week, but NOT everyday!

  3. Lastly, dont be too harsh on yourself. Its all about the journey and the experience. Rushing the process wont help you. So be sure to have a proper diet!
